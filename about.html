Our Story

Country Gardens is a plant nursery and landscaping company based in Kennesaw, Georgia. In 1990, Frank Zorda turned his love for gardening into a business. Over the years, they developed many loyal customers and gained positive reputation around town. Many of the customers are attracted to the coziness of the garden and personal touch they couldn't receive at larger nurseries. Each yard is unique, and Frank himself works directly with each customer to ensure their vision of their yard becomes reality.
